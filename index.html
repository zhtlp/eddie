Hello, I'm Eddie Zhang

Student, builder, and global citizen driven by restless curiosity. I learn through action—designing and programming robots, running small experiments, competing as an athlete, and documenting what I discover. Setbacks, including recovering from injury, have taught me patience and grit. Travel and languages broaden my perspective and empathy. In teams I listen first, think rigorously, and lead with integrity. I care about problems that matter and the people behind them, and I try to turn ideas into useful, human‑centered outcomes. Above all, I bring discipline, kindness, and teamwork to every project—learning fast, iterating thoughtfully, and helping those around me do their best work.
