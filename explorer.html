Explorer

I love this world.
But why?
Not for its breathtaking landscapes,
Not for the countless delicacies scattered across the globe,
Not to post on social media and collect likes…
………………………………
I love this world
because of my grandfather.

My grandfather was a highly respected senior seafarer. He led fleets to Australia, Egypt, Canada, the United Kingdom, Brazil… He had sailed almost every corner of the world!

Since I was a child, I have listened, wide-eyed, to his tales of fascinating places and adventures.

And so,
I want to see the world for myself.
I want to study abroad.
I want to explore this world.
I want to set foot on the lands he once walked, to witness the rising sun, feel the wind brushing my face, and watch the rain fall…

My beloved grandfather loved this world with all his heart. And because of him, I love it even more.

This piece is dedicated to my dearly beloved late grandfather.
